Enhancing Risk Prediction in Cardiovascular Health

This project aims to perform discriminant analysis on Mr. John Hughes' MHR dataset, employing statistical techniques to identify key variables that differentiate between distinct risk levels. The project employs the Synthetic Minority Over-sampling Technique (SMOTE) to ensure balanced representation of risk levels within the dataset, contributing to more robust and accurate predictions in cardiovascular health.
